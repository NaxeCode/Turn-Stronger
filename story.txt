PLOT----------
The protagonist, Tru, is a newly abandoned failed experiment who gets taken in by an old failed experiement who has a lot of knowledge on current events.
This old experiment's name is Noah, and his mission is to eradicate the current row of experiments created to destroy the seal to escape to the human world.
One day while Tru was out exploring, a raid happens on the failed experiment kindergarten and Noah is brutally beaten up and taken away for questioning.
Tru comes out to find Noah's sunglasses and their hide out destroyed.
Tru must set off on an adventure to save Noah and uncover the mysteries of this world and why there is a barrier to begin with.
